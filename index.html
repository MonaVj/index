How is climate change affecting migration in Central America Northern Triangle?

Forced migration due to climate change will present one of the most severe challenges to the resilience of communities forced to migrate as well as to local and national governments. The Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change (IPCC) predicts that 150 million people will be displaced by 2050.
